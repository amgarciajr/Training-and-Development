The Four Layers — Architecture Overview

EEP Module 1
Foundations of Engagement Excellence
Layer 1
Signal Intelligence
Detect early indicators of client sentiment, risk, and relationship health before they escalate.
Layer 2
Ownership Clarity
Establish who is accountable for each client relationship before delivery motions begin.
Layer 3
Communication Discipline
Apply the Three Tests to every client communication to ensure clarity, alignment, and trust.
Layer 4
Outcome Validation
Client articulates the value received before formal engagement close — not assumed, confirmed.
Architectural Principle
The sequencing is causal, not merely organizational — each layer depends on the one before it.
You cannot enforce Communication Discipline without first establishing Ownership Clarity. You cannot validate Outcomes without first communicating with discipline.
