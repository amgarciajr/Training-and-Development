EEP Certification Path · Section 1.2

Three Certification Levels — Overview

Sequential Advancement
L1
EEP Practitioner
4–6 Weeks
Audience: All onboarding, client-facing team members
Authority Granted
CEES Session participation
Scenario contribution
L2
EEP Lead
6–9 Months from L1
Audience: L1 practitioners with 6+ months active delivery
Authority Granted
Onboard new practitioners
Co-facilitate new hire sessions
L3
EEP Trainer
3–6 Months from L2 Nomination-Based
Audience: L2 Leads nominated as program stewards
Authority Granted
Certify L1 practitioners
Contributing playbook author; quarterly program evolution
Sequential Advancement Required: Each level builds on the prior — completion of the previous certification is a prerequisite for advancement. There are no shortcuts or level-skipping within the EEP framework.
