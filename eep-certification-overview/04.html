EEP Certification Path · Section 1.2.1

Level 1 — EEP Practitioner Requirements

Target completion: 4–6 weeks from onboarding start  ·  All client-facing team members
R1
EDEv4 Introduction Session
Live 90-minute session covering engagement delivery foundations. Async recordings do not satisfy this requirement. 90 min · Live only
R2
CEEDS Onboarding Session
Attend the Client Engagement Excellence Delivery System onboarding session introducing core delivery components. 90 min · Facilitated
R3
CE Framework Deep-Dive
Structured session applying the Client Engagement Framework to real delivery scenarios and practitioner contexts. 60 min · Applied
R4
Written Scenario Assessment
Two scenarios scored against the rubric. Minimum 80% required to pass. One retake permitted after a 72-hour waiting period. 80% min · 1 retake
R5
Supervised CEES Session
Demonstrate signal language, no-blame framing, and at least one substantive lesson contribution in a live CEES session. Observed · Behavioral
