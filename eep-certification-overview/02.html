Certification Philosophy

EEP · Section 1.1
“

The program certifies what practitioners do in the field, not what they know in the room.

EEP Certification Path  ·  Philosophy of Certification
”
Behavioral Standard
Certification measures demonstrated conduct — not course completion or knowledge recall.
Prerequisite
Session completion opens the door to assessment — it does not constitute certification itself.
Evidence-Based
All evidence must be observable and verifiable — self-report is never accepted as sufficient proof.
Sustained Practice
Advancement requires repeated demonstration over time — a single strong performance is not enough.
