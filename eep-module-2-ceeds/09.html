EEP · Module 2 — CEEDS

Module 2 Summary & Next Steps

Four Pillars
Explicit trade-offs the team commits to — not aspirational values. Each pillar defines a concrete behavioral standard the team holds itself to across every engagement.
Five Operating Motions
Recurring practice that translates pillar commitments into delivery. These are not one-time events — they are the repeating cadence that keeps the program alive in the team.
CEES Sessions
Bi-weekly, 45-minute team learning sessions using real scenarios — the primary mechanism for building judgment, calibrating standards, and reinforcing signal language.
Signal Language
The vocabulary of the program — observable client behaviors, not status updates. "The client seems happy" is status. "The client asked three clarifying questions" is a signal.
