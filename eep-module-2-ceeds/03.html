The Four CEEDS Pillars

EEP Module 2 · Explicit Trade-Offs the Team Commits To
Pillar 1 · Trade-Off
Customer Experience Over Technical Perfection
When clarity conflicts with elegance, choose what the client can understand and act on — not what is technically impressive.
01
Pillar 2 · Trade-Off
Clarity Over Complexity
The measure of a communication is comprehension, not sophistication. If it cannot be understood, it has not been communicated.
02
Pillar 3 · Trade-Off
Ownership Without Boundaries
Ownership is volunteered, not assigned by default. Team members step in before being asked rather than waiting for explicit direction.
03
Pillar 4 · Trade-Off
Signal Over Opinion
Engagement health is assessed through observable indicators and evidence, not individual intuition or subjective feeling.
