EEP Module 4 · Mastery Indicators

What Mastery Looks Like

Mastery is a behavioral state — the EEP standard operates without reference to the standard.
Signal Language
EEP signal vocabulary is natural speech, not a framework consciously applied during delivery conversations.
Ownership Gaps
Practitioners identify and close ownership gaps proactively, before being asked or prompted by leadership.
Three Tests
Communications pass the Three Tests automatically, without deliberate step-by-step application of the model.
Trust Architecture
Trust-building moments are designed into engagement plans from kickoff, not added reactively mid-engagement.
