The Self-Sustaining Program

Four mechanisms that sustain EEP without dependency on any single leader

Certification Hierarchy
Level 3 Trainers certify Level 1 Practitioners, distributing program authority downward over time without central dependency.
Playbook Evolution
The trainer cohort updates the playbook quarterly, embedding real delivery learnings directly into the living program standard.
Scenario Library Growth
New scenarios sourced from live engagements keep practice material current, field-tested, and continuously relevant to real delivery.
Adjacent Team Adoption
Certified trainers expand the program to new teams independently — growth is not gated by the founding team's involvement.
