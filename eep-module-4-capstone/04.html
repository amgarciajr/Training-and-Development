Leading EEP Across Teams

Advanced practitioners extend EEP adoption beyond their own team through four core leadership behaviors.
Modeling
Demonstrate EEP behaviors visibly in every delivery conversation, not just in formal session contexts where performance is expected.
Coaching
Develop practitioners through structured feedback loops and deliberate observation — not just answering ad-hoc questions as they arise.
Sustaining
Maintain program integrity over time by identifying and correcting behavioral drift before it becomes normalized as team culture.
Expanding
Introduce EEP to adjacent teams and support their adoption independently — without requiring the founding team to lead every implementation.
