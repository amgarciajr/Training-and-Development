Assessment Scope & Methodology

The Five-Day Assessment Sprint — structured discovery across all security domains
Day 1
Tenant Orientation & Stakeholder Interviews
Tenant orientation & emergency access verification Licensing review & baseline documentation Interviews: CISO, Identity lead, Compliance lead
Day 2
Entra ID Deep-Dive
PIM status & Access Reviews cadence Entitlement Management & guest governance Identity lifecycle & service principal audit
Day 3
Conditional Access Analysis
Policy export & sign-in log review Legacy auth breakdown & Named Location review Gap identification vs. baseline requirements
Day 4
Purview Posture
Label taxonomy review & DLP coverage Insider Risk configuration assessment Compliance Manager score & eDiscovery readiness
Day 5
Defender / Sentinel & Synthesis
Defender XDR / Sentinel review Operational model & data synthesis Maturity scoring & findings readout prep
Delivery Standard: Each day targets a distinct security domain — findings from all five days feed directly into the maturity scoring framework and prioritized 90-day roadmap.
