1
Initial
No formal process in place; controls are absent or unknown
Remediation is reactive only — no proactive posture
High likelihood of active exploitation risk present
2
Developing
Some controls exist but not enforced consistently
Significant gaps remain; policy exists on paper only
Inconsistent application across teams and workloads
3
Defined
Baseline Target — No Active Exploitation Risk
Core controls in place and enforced; documentation exists
Gaps are known, tracked, and actively managed
No active exploitation risk from baseline control gaps
4
Managed
Controls monitored, measured, and metrics available
Exceptions tracked and time-limited with clear ownership
Process is repeatable, owned, and consistently applied
5
Optimized
Controls automated and self-healing where possible
Continuous review cadence; metrics drive improvement
Threat-informed posture with proactive adaptation
Scores represent a baseline — not a grade. The question is: "Where are we starting from?" Security Modernization Field Guide § 3.4 · Delivery Confidential
