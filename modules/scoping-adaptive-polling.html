Scoping & Adaptive Polling

Overview

The six steps before Day One determine most CPE outcomes. Scoping, recap, pre-poll, and adaptive polling ensure clarity, alignment, and real-time course correction.

Key Concepts

  • Scoping Call — discover the real business problem.
  • Recap Email — written alignment before delivery.
  • Pre-Poll — expectations, expertise, objectives.
  • Live Poll — adapt in real time.
  • Exceptions — SCOP, Lab Assist only.
