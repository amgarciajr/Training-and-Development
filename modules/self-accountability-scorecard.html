Self-Accountability Scorecard

Overview

A 12-criterion scorecard for grading yourself against the standard you set, with evidence and trend tracking every 30 days.

Key Concepts

  • 1–5 scale — absent → embedded.
  • Evidence required — for every score — no evidence, no score.
  • Five pillars — CX, clarity, ownership, signal, accountability.
  • Quarterly narrative — where the standard held or slipped.
  • Drift Watch — identify quiet regressions early.
