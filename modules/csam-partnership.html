CSAM Partnership

Overview

The CSAM is your closest ally. They own the relationship; you own the outcome. Partnership rhythm determines trust, pipeline, and future routing.

Key Concepts

  • Make the CSAM look prepared — no surprises.
  • Signal-over-status updates — communicate what changes decisions.
  • Disagree in private, align in public — maintain a unified front with customers.
  • Outcome Owner call — shared between EDE and CSAM.
  • Weekly CSAM check-in — maintain consistent partnership rhythm.
