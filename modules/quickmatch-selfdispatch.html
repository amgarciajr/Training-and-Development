Quick Match & Self-Dispatch

Overview

Quick Match and self-dispatch require disciplined queue management. Dispatch type changes entry, not the standard.

Key Concepts

  • Dispatch taxonomy — proactive, Quick Match, RMOT, self-dispatch.
  • First 48 hours — CSAM contact, scoping, recap, pre-poll.
  • RMOT — shared pool, single Outcome Owner.
  • Debunk — short engagements still require polling.
  • Weekly Quick Match check — maintain queue discipline and visibility.
