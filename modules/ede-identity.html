EDE Identity

Overview

EDE Identity defines who you are at the customer's table: the deepest technical expertise applied to the hardest business problems.

Key Concepts

  • Role evolution — PFE → CE → CSA → EDE.
  • Mission — outcomes, not deliverables.
  • Trusted-advisor stance — opinions, not options.
  • Three Tests — Repeat-Back, Stakes, Next-Step.
  • Anti-patterns — hedging, Microsoft-speak, scope creep.
